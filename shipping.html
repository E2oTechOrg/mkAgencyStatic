Shipping Policy

At MK Agency, we are committed to ensuring timely, safe, and reliable delivery of our grocery and export products. This Shipping Policy outlines our practices and customer responsibilities regarding order processing, delivery, and logistics.

Order Processing
  • Domestic orders are typically processed within 24–48 hours of payment confirmation.
  • Export orders are processed according to agreed schedules, documentation requirements, and shipping arrangements.
  • Bulk or special orders may require additional processing time.
Delivery Timelines
  • Domestic deliveries: 2–7 business days, depending on location and courier service.
  • Export deliveries: Based on shipping mode (air, sea, or land) and customs clearance timelines.
  • Unexpected delays (holidays, strikes, weather, customs, logistics issues) may affect delivery schedules.
Shipping Methods & Partners
  • We use trusted logistics providers, courier services, and freight forwarders for safe delivery.
  • Customers may request specific shipping partners for export orders (subject to availability and additional costs).
  • Tracking details are shared once the order is dispatched.
Shipping Charges
  • Domestic charges are calculated at checkout based on weight, size, and delivery location.
  • Export shipping depends on volume, freight method, customs duty, and destination country.
  • Customs duties, import taxes, or local charges at the buyer’s end are the customer’s responsibility.
Packaging & Handling
  • All products are securely packed to maintain freshness and prevent damage.
  • Export consignments follow international packaging and safety standards.
  • Bulk orders may use wooden crates, pallets, or reinforced packaging.
Customs & Documentation (Export Only)
  • We provide export documentation including invoices, packing lists, certificates (if required), and shipping bills.
  • Buyers are responsible for import permits, licenses, and compliance with local regulations.
  • Customs clearance delays are beyond our control, though we provide full documentation support.
Insurance & Liability
  • Domestic orders are shipped at customer’s risk unless insured.
  • Export orders can be insured upon request; costs are borne by the buyer unless otherwise agreed.
  • MK Agency is not liable for loss, theft, or damage once handed to the courier/freight forwarder unless insured.
Delivery Attempts & Non-Delivery
  • For domestic orders, failed delivery after multiple attempts may result in return, with re-shipping costs charged to the customer.
  • For exports, undeliverable shipments due to incorrect details or refusal will incur return freight and penalty charges.
Damaged or Missing Goods
  • Customers must inspect packages upon delivery.
  • Damages or shortages must be reported within 48 hours with proof (photo/video).
  • Claims are processed only if reported within the specified timeframe.
Force Majeure
  • MK Agency is not responsible for delays or non-delivery caused by natural disasters, political instability, strikes, or other events beyond our control.
