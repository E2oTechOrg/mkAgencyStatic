Privacy Policy

At MK Agency, we respect and protect the privacy of our customers, partners, and website users. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and safeguard your personal information.

Information We Collect
  • Name, phone number, email, and postal address
  • Billing and shipping information
  • Payment details (processed via secure gateways; we do not store sensitive financial data)
  • Order history and purchase preferences
  • Website usage data (cookies, IP address, browser type)
How We Use Your Information
  • Processing and delivering orders
  • Communicating order updates, invoices, and shipping details
  • Responding to queries and customer support requests
  • Sending promotional offers, newsletters, and updates (if you opt-in)
  • Improving our products, services, and website performance
  • Compliance with legal and regulatory requirements
Data Sharing & Disclosure
  • We do not sell or rent customer data to third parties.
  • Data may be shared with trusted partners (payment processors, courier companies, customs agents) solely to complete transactions.
  • Information may be disclosed if required by law or to protect our rights.
Data Security
  • We use industry-standard security measures, including encryption, firewalls, and secure servers.
  • Sensitive payment info is processed through certified gateways.
  • Access to customer data is restricted to authorized personnel.
Cookies & Tracking
  • Our site may use cookies to enhance browsing and personalize services.
  • Cookies can be disabled, but some features may not work properly.
International Data Transfers
  • Export orders may require sharing info with shipping agents, customs, and logistics providers.
  • All transfers comply with relevant regulations (e.g., GDPR).
Customer Rights
  • Access stored personal data
  • Request corrections or updates
  • Request deletion (subject to legal/transactional needs)
  • Opt out of promotional communications
Data Retention
  • Data is retained only as long as needed for transactions, legal compliance, or service improvements.
  • When no longer needed, it is securely deleted or anonymized.
Third-Party Links
  • Our site may link to third-party websites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices.
Policy Updates
  • MK Agency may update this Privacy Policy anytime. Updates will be posted here, and continued use means acceptance.
