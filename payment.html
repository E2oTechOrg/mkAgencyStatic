Payment Policy

At MK Agency, we believe in transparency and fairness in all financial transactions. The following terms outline our payment guidelines for retail, wholesale, and export customers.

Accepted Payment Methods
  • Retail customers can pay via UPI, debit/credit cards, net banking, wallets, or Cash on Delivery (where available).
  • Bulk and export customers may use bank transfers, demand drafts, or Letters of Credit (LC).
  • International payments can be made via SWIFT transfer or other agreed modes.
Advance Payments
  • Retail orders must be 100% prepaid unless Cash on Delivery (COD) is offered.
  • Wholesale/export orders generally require 50% advance payment at order confirmation and balance before dispatch, unless otherwise agreed.
Credit Terms (B2B & Export Clients)
  • Credit facilities may be extended to long-term, verified customers on a case-to-case basis.
  • Payment due dates must be strictly adhered to, and overdue accounts may attract interest/late fees.
  • Failure to clear dues may result in suspension of services and future order restrictions.
Currency & Forex Handling
  • Domestic payments must be made in INR (Indian Rupees).
  • Export payments may be accepted in USD, EUR, GBP, or other mutually agreed currencies.
  • All currency conversion charges, bank fees, and forex fluctuations are to be borne by the buyer.
Taxes & Duties
  • All domestic orders are subject to GST as per government regulations.
  • Export orders are subject to customs duties, import taxes, and clearance charges in the destination country, payable by the buyer.
Payment Confirmation
  • Orders are processed only after successful payment confirmation.
  • For bank transfers, customers must share transaction details to avoid delays.
Non-Payment & Default
  • MK Agency reserves the right to withhold shipments if payments are not received as per agreed terms.
  • Persistent default may lead to account termination and legal action, if necessary.
Refunds & Adjustments
  • Any excess payment or adjustments (for returned/canceled goods) will be refunded or credited to the customer’s account within 7–10 working days.
  • Refunds for export clients will be handled as per international banking timelines.
Dispute Handling
  • In case of any payment disputes, customers are requested to contact our support team.
  • Disputes will be resolved in line with applicable laws and mutually agreed trade terms (e.g., Incoterms for exports).
