Cancellation & Refund Policy

At MK Agency, we strive to provide quality products and a smooth shopping experience. This policy explains the conditions under which cancellations, returns, and refunds are accepted.

Order Cancellation
  • Customers may cancel retail orders within 24 hours of purchase, provided the order has not been processed or shipped.
  • For bulk/export orders, cancellations must be made before dispatch and require written confirmation.
  • Once an order is shipped, cancellation requests will not be accepted.
  • MK Agency reserves the right to cancel orders in case of:
    • Suspected fraudulent activity
    • Incorrect billing/shipping information
    • Product unavailability or force majeure events
Non-Cancellable Orders
  • Customized, bulk, or export orders once confirmed cannot be canceled unless there is a major product or shipment issue.
  • Perishable or short-shelf-life grocery products are non-cancellable once shipped.
Return Policy
  • Returns are accepted only if:
    • The wrong product was delivered
    • The product was damaged during transit
    • The product is expired or defective upon delivery
  • Customers must notify us within 7 working days of delivery with proof (photo/video).
  • Returned products must be unused, unopened, and in original packaging.
Refund Policy
  • Approved refunds will be issued to the original payment method within 7–10 business days.
  • Refunds are not applicable for:
    • Change of mind
    • Incorrect product selection by the customer
    • Products damaged due to improper storage/handling after delivery
  • For bulk/export shipments, refunds will only be processed after verification and inspection reports (if applicable).
Replacement Option
  • Instead of refunds, customers may request product replacement (subject to stock availability).
  • If the error was on our part, MK Agency will cover replacement shipping costs.
Shipping & Handling Charges
  • Shipping charges, customs duties, and taxes are non-refundable.
  • For export returns, buyers must bear return freight, customs clearance, and related costs unless the error was solely MK Agency’s responsibility.
Dispute Resolution
  • Any disputes related to cancellations or refunds will be resolved under Indian law.
  • For export orders, disputes will be handled under mutually agreed international trade terms.
